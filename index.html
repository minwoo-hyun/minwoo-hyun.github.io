About Me

Moving Towards a Sustainable Future

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Economics at the University of California, Santa Barbara. My research interests include the economics of the energy and environment and policy issues relevant to health, inequality, and development. I am currently studying the local fiscal conditions and labor reallocation costs of coal decline, crime and health impacts in coal-rich communities, and the welfare consequences of pollution alerts.
